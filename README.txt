Welcome to the Lunaris! 

This modification aims to add a new dimension to minecraft.

Traveling to the Lunaris.
1. Build a nether portal out of Chiseled Stone Brick. 
2. Craft a moondial using 4 gold, 4 lapiz lazuli, and 1 diamond.
3. Right click the inside of the portal frame with the moondial in your main hand.

I made this modification as my Capstone Project within a time span of 14 weeks. 
Feel free to leave any bug reports or suggestions. 

Finally I do hope you enjoy your stay in this new adventure.
